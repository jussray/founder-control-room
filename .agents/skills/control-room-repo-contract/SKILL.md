# Founder Control Room Repository Contract

Read `GLOBAL_AI.md` and `AGENTS.md` first. This skill makes their requirements explicit for connected-tool work.

## Before acting

Establish:

- the canonical repository, branch, and exact commit;
- the project and data trust boundary;
- the founder-approved scope and the next separate approval gate;
- authoritative source, observed evidence, and freshness;
- privacy, security, provider, database, deployment, spending, and rollback impact;
- the smallest coherent implementation and its proof plan.

## Non-negotiable rules

- Founder Control Room remains separate from Se’kret Bip and every other product database, credential set, and private-content boundary.
- Connected tools provide scoped capabilities; they do not carry authority from one action to another.
- Never represent a queued, skipped, runner-less, stale, wrong-commit, or logless check as passing evidence.
- Never expose credentials, raw private product content, teen or parent data, applicant-private data, or provider payloads in design, test, or model fixtures.
- Repository writes, database migrations, merges, deployments, rollbacks, secret access, spending, destructive actions, and external communication remain separately gated.
- Preserve provider independence, exact-head verification, audit history, and a concrete rollback path.

## Completion report

Record files and behavior changed, exact commit, checks and provenance, failures/skips, privacy and security impact, provider and data impact, rollback, unresolved risks, and the next approval gate.
