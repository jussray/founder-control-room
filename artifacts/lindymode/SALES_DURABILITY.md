# Lindy Sales Durability

Durable sales systems are built from primitives that survive tools, channels, models, and providers:

- verified customer problem;
- clear decision owner;
- useful offer with explicit scope;
- honest proof and limitations;
- reversible next commitment;
- reliable fulfillment;
- support and recovery;
- retention and referral earned through outcomes;
- simple records that can move between providers.

Avoid dependence on one CRM, ad platform, model, checkout provider, social network, or proprietary scoring system. Store evidence, decisions, consent, offer versions, and outcomes in portable formats behind adapters.

The durable metric hierarchy is:

1. customer outcome and trust;
2. retention and complaint/refund quality;
3. gross-margin and cash-conversion quality;
4. qualified conversion and cycle time;
5. activity metrics last.

Complex persuasion machinery is not a moat. A repeatedly useful offer, credible proof, operational reliability, and customer memory are.
