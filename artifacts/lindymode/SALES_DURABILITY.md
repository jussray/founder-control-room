# Lindy Sales Durability

Durable sales systems are built from verified customer need, clear decision authority, useful offers, honest proof and limits, reversible commitments, reliable fulfillment, support and recovery, retained outcomes, and portable records.

Avoid dependence on one CRM, ad platform, model, checkout provider, social network, or proprietary scoring system. Store offer versions, evidence, consent, decisions, and outcomes in portable formats behind adapters.

Metric priority:

1. customer outcome and trust;
2. retention and complaint/refund quality;
3. gross-margin and cash-conversion quality;
4. qualified conversion and cycle time;
5. activity metrics last.

Complex persuasion machinery is not a moat. Repeatedly useful offers, credible proof, operational reliability, and customer memory are.
