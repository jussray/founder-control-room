# Bill Gates Artifact — Sales Platform Leverage

## Platform thesis

Treat sales intelligence as reusable infrastructure, not isolated copywriting. Every project should answer who has the problem and authority, what outcome is offered, what proof supports it, what disqualifiers apply, what reversible commitment comes next, and what evidence determines continuation or shutdown.

## Shared leverage

Founder Control Room can standardize offer versions, evidence freshness, qualification, objections, approvals, outcomes, retention, and rollback while every project preserves its own catalog, customer data, vendors, credentials, fulfillment, pricing authority, and voice.

The reusable layer remains provider-neutral through CRM and commerce adapters, versioned schemas, project-scoped permissions, immutable decisions, aggregate portfolio metrics without raw customer-content centralization, and explicit communication, spend, pricing, checkout, and deployment gates.

## Ten-year rule

Optimize for simple contracts, portable data, replaceable providers, documented deprecations, auditability, and human-controlled execution. Models and channels change. Verified need, honest proof, operational reliability, and earned trust remain.
