# OODA Sales Execution Loop

**Observe:** exact offer, audience, catalog, pricing authority, proof, funnel, fulfillment capacity, support load, retention, refund and complaint signals, market conditions, and current repository/runtime state.

**Orient:** separate fact, inference, and unknown; complete 5W1H; apply `/devil` premise attack, Lindy durability, L99 authority, and project-specific privacy and catalog boundaries.

**Decide:** choose one measurable hypothesis and the smallest reversible next commitment. Define success, failure, kill criteria, owner, evidence deadline, and rollback.

**Act:** draft or execute only the specifically approved action. Analysis approval does not authorize outreach, pricing, spending, publishing, checkout, or deployment.

**Loop:** re-observe customer response, delivery quality, margin, complaints, refunds, retention, and unintended effects. Continue, revise, pause, or terminate based on current proof.
