# OODA Sales Execution Loop

## Observe

Inspect the exact offer, audience, catalog, pricing authority, proof, funnel, fulfillment capacity, support load, retention, refund/complaint signals, market conditions, and current repository/runtime state.

## Orient

Separate fact, inference, and unknown. Complete 5W1H. Apply `/devil` premise attack, Lindy durability screen, L99 authority map, and project-specific privacy/catalog boundaries.

## Decide

Choose one measurable hypothesis and the smallest reversible next commitment. Define success, failure, kill criteria, owner, evidence deadline, and rollback before execution.

## Act

Draft or execute only the specifically approved action. Preserve idempotency and audit records. Do not let analysis approval authorize outreach, pricing, spending, publishing, checkout, or deployment.

## Loop

Re-observe customer response, delivery quality, margin, complaints, refunds, retention, and unintended effects. Update the evidence packet; do not rewrite history. Continue, revise, pause, or terminate based on current proof.
