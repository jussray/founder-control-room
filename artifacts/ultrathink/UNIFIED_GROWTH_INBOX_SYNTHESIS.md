# ULTRATHINK — Unified Growth Inbox Synthesis

## Reality

Juss wants one system that combines inbound and approved outbound business
conversations across Instagram Direct, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Telegram,
email, SMS/MMS, voice, Viber, and webchat to generate leads, sales, sponsors,
bookings, and retained customers.

The durable opportunity is not ten independent bots. It is one provider-independent
conversation, consent, qualification, dispatch, attribution, and audit protocol with
channel-specific adapters.

Google Business Messages is retired and must not receive a new adapter.

## Premise attack

"Autopilot" fails when it means unrestricted cold outreach. That approach creates
spam complaints, account restrictions, carrier filtering, consent disputes, legal
exposure, brand damage, and low-quality pipeline data.

The useful automation target is:

- inbound capture;
- identity and project resolution;
- consent and suppression;
- approved FAQ assistance;
- lead qualification;
- draft generation;
- founder-controlled sequences;
- attribution and collected-revenue evidence.

## Decision

Build a Unified Growth Inbox in staged authority levels:

1. observe only;
2. draft only;
3. approved inbound assistance;
4. founder-approved permission-valid sequences;
5. trusted narrow rules after production and legal/policy evidence.

There is no unrestricted autonomous cold-outreach level.

## Lawful-money invariant

A dispatch is denied unless every required decision is `allow`:

```text
project + brand
+ sender identity
+ recipient identity
+ purpose
+ consent evidence
+ suppression
+ platform window or approved template
+ jurisdiction
+ quiet hours
+ sender/campaign/telemarketer registration
+ approved content and substantiated claims
+ frequency/budget/kill controls
+ audit and idempotency
```

Unknown, conflicting, expired, or missing evidence fails closed.

Only `payment_collected` counts as recognized revenue. Waitlists, leads, bookings,
invoices, forecasts, and sponsor pledges remain separate funnel states.

## Pennsylvania gate

Outbound sales calling remains disabled until qualified counsel resolves whether
registration or an exemption applies and the system can prove applicable bonding,
Pennsylvania and national Do Not Call screening, internal suppression, calling hours,
required disclosures, and separate rules for artificial/prerecorded/synthetic voice,
recording, and transcription.

## Se’kret Bip boundary

The growth system may use separately consented waitlist, founding-preview, sponsor,
referral, launch-update, and communication-preference data. It may not ingest or
exploit journals, voice notes, private companion chats, safety events, emotional
state, health data, parent-visible content, private activity, or vulnerability.

## First implementation

First-party webchat in `draft_only` mode is the recommended first live adapter:

- user initiated;
- first-party branding and consent;
- direct connection from splash and waiting-list surfaces;
- no dependence on external app review;
- complete proof of normalization, consent, qualification, drafting, handoff,
  attribution, suppression, and audit before adding more complex channels.

## Current branch proof boundary

This branch adds the parent skill specialization, private plan, compliance gate,
machine-readable channel registry, provider-neutral TypeScript contracts, agent
activation, and an executable static verifier.

It does not connect credentials, receive or send real messages, call anyone, launch
a campaign, apply a migration, deploy, spend, merge, change billing, or authorize
pricing and discounts.
