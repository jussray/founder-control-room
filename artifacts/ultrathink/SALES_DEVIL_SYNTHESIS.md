# ULTRATHINK — Sales + Devil Synthesis

The strongest commercial system does not maximize persuasion. It maximizes the probability that the right buyer receives a useful, deliverable, well-supported offer while the wrong buyer is disqualified early.

`/sales` supplies constructive motion: qualification, value thesis, offer, proof, next commitment, fulfillment, retention, and measurement.

`/devil` supplies destructive testing: counter-thesis, incentive analysis, objection integrity, failure simulation, kill criteria, and rollback.

Together they produce a controlled loop:

`5W1H → observe → qualify → premise attack → durable offer → system/authority map → plan attack → founder gate → smallest action → outcome evidence → OODA loop`

The system must reward:

- truthful disqualification;
- proof quality over copy quality;
- retained customer value over acquisition volume;
- gross-margin and fulfillment quality over topline vanity;
- explicit unknowns over confident fabrication;
- portable customer and offer records over provider lock-in;
- founder-controlled execution over autonomous outreach.

The commercial moat is the compound interaction of Story, Quality, Care, Proof, operational reliability, and memory—not an AI-generated script.
