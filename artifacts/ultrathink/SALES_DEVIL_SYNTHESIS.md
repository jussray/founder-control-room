# ULTRATHINK — Sales + Devil Synthesis

The strongest commercial system does not maximize persuasion. It maximizes the probability that the right buyer receives a useful, deliverable, well-supported offer while the wrong buyer is disqualified early.

`/sales` supplies constructive motion: qualification, value thesis, offer, proof, next commitment, fulfillment, retention, and measurement.

`/devil` supplies destructive testing: counter-thesis, incentive analysis, objection integrity, failure simulation, kill criteria, and rollback.

Operating chain:

`5W1H → observe → qualify → premise attack → durable offer → L99 authority map → plan attack → founder gate → smallest action → outcome evidence → OODA`

Reward truthful disqualification, proof over copy, retained value over acquisition volume, fulfillment and margin quality over topline vanity, explicit unknowns over fabrication, portable records over provider lock-in, and founder-controlled execution over autonomous outreach.
